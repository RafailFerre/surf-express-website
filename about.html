About Us

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in cargo transportation

Founded in 2022 by Boris Gordienko

Surf Express began as a small logistics company with just two trucks and a big vision. Our founder, Boris Gordienko, a former professional surfer and logistics expert, combined his passion for movement with his business acumen to create a company that would revolutionize cargo transportation.

Boris named the company "Surf Express" as a metaphor - just as surfers ride waves efficiently across the ocean, our trucks would efficiently transport goods across the country. The surfing background also inspired our company values: adaptability, flow, and riding the waves of opportunity.

Our Growth

Starting with local deliveries in 2022, we quickly expanded our services nationwide thanks to our commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction. Today, Surf Express operates a fleet of over 50 modern trucks and employs more than 100 dedicated professionals.

Our Mission

We exist to make cargo transportation simple, reliable, and efficient for businesses of all sizes. Our goal is to be the most trusted logistics partner for companies who value their goods and their customers' satisfaction.
